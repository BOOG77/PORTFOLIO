Now I've built full sophisticated programs like college databases, library systems, full android video games, and so many more things that I couldn't possibly fit them all into one list nowadays. If I could go back and tell myself anything it would be to trust the process, programming isn't a simple thing and requires understanding a huge amount of concepts.
